SIENNA MARIE

INTERIOR DESIGN

Interior design shaped by light, material restraint and considered detail.

Sienna Marie is an Auckland-based interior designer working across residential interiors, styling and spatial concepts.

Her work is grounded in atmosphere, with a focus on light, proportion and material restraint. She is interested in how spaces are experienced day to day — how they feel, move and settle over time.

Her experience spans residential projects, bespoke furniture and interior styling, informing a well-rounded and hands-on approach to design. She works in a considered and detail-driven way, balancing clarity with warmth.
