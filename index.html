Sienna Marie Interior Design

Interior design shaped by light, material restraint and considered detail.

Sienna Marie is an Auckland-based interior designer working across residential interiors, styling and spatial concepts.

Her work is grounded in atmosphere, proportion and materiality — creating spaces that feel refined, calm and quietly elevated.

With a background spanning interior design, furniture, styling and high-end residential detailing, Sienna approaches each project with a focus on spatial flow, natural light and thoughtful finishes.
