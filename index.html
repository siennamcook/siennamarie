Sienna Marie

Sienna Marie Interior Design is shaped by a love of light, material restraint and considered detail.

Working across residential interiors, styling and spatial concepts, Sienna creates spaces that feel refined, warm and quietly elevated.

Her work balances old and new — preserving character where it matters, while introducing contemporary detailing, tonal material palettes and thoughtful spatial solutions for modern living.

Recent work includes the interior direction of a restored Ponsonby villa, where compact planning, natural light, integrated storage and expressive stone were used to create a home that feels both functional and highly considered.
